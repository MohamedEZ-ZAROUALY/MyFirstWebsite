Chronology of most important events
1922

The foundation of the club

The club was founded in Mars 12th of 1922.

1936

First title

The club won its first title in the Campeonato regional Hispano-Marroqui.

1942

A second title

The club won its second title in the Campeonato regional Hispano-Marroqui.

1943

Third gold

The club won its third title in the Campeonato regional Hispano-Marroqui.

1948

A last title before independance

The club won its fourth title in the Campeonato regional Hispano-Marroqui.

1951

Memory in El Copa Del Rey

The club reached the quarter finals of El Copa Del Rey to face FC Barcelona.

1952

The only non sapnish team to play in La Liga

The club played the 1951/52 season in La Liga ( The first division of spanish football competition ) to be the only non spanish team in the history to play in La Liga.

1956

First Moroccan Botola

The club played in the first Moroccan football competition organized after the independance to be the only representant of the northern region.

2008

First foreign Arab appearance

The club played in the 2008 Arab Champions League in its first foreign appearance.

2012

First Botola Pro

In the first season of the long waited professional Botola ,the club won its first title after independance to be the first club to win the professional title.

2013

First foreign African appearance

The club played in the 2013 African Champions League in its first continental appearance.

2014

A second professional title and an international appearance

The club won its second title in the history of profession in Morocco to represents the country in the Mundialito.

2015

A shining continental participation

The club succeded in its second African participation in CAF Champions League to reach the Quarters Finals.
