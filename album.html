DID YOU KNOW THAT?

The kit of the club which is a red and white shirt with blue shorts and socks is inspired of the colours of the club of Atletico Madrid.  

The inspiration came from the founders of the team who were some supporters of Atletico Madrid who settled in Tetouan during the period of the Spanish protectorate of Morocco.
